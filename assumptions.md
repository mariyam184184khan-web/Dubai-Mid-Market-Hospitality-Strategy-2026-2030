# Assumptions

## Base Operating Inputs

-   Number of Rooms: 250
-   Occupancy Rate: 72%
-   ADR: AED 450
-   OTA Share: 55%
-   OTA Commission: 20%
-   Operating Cost Rate: 78% of net revenue

## Sensitivity Levers

-   ADR Uplift: +2% and +4%
-   OTA Share Reduction: 5 percentage points
-   Cost Efficiency Improvement: 4%

## Scenario Definitions

### Base Case

Current market structure assumptions maintained.

### Optimistic Case

-   ADR uplift of +2%
-   OTA share reduced by 5pp
-   4% operating efficiency gain

### Risk Case

-   ADR decline of -2%
-   OTA share increase of 5pp
-   3% cost inflation

All assumptions are illustrative and aligned with publicly available
industry benchmarks.
