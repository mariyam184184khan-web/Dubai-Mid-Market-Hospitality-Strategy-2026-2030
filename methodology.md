# Methodology

## Overview

This strategic advisory case follows a structured consulting methodology
combining financial modeling, competitive benchmarking, and economic
alignment assessment.

## Analytical Framework

### 1. Sector Context Analysis

-   Review of tourism recovery trends
-   ADR and occupancy benchmarking
-   Regional competitive positioning

### 2. Revenue-Cost Diagnostic

-   OTA dependency impact assessment
-   ADR elasticity and sensitivity testing
-   Cost structure decomposition

### 3. Scenario-Based Financial Modeling

-   Base, Optimistic, and Risk case scenarios
-   Sensitivity testing on ADR and distribution mix
-   EBITDA margin impact modeling

### 4. Strategic Design Framework

-   Revenue optimization
-   Digital transformation strategy
-   Experience differentiation
-   Operational restructuring

## Modeling Logic

Financial modeling assumes publicly benchmarked industry ranges and
applies structured scenario analysis rather than proprietary data.

## Limitations

All financial figures are illustrative and based on reasonable industry
assumptions derived from public domain sources.
